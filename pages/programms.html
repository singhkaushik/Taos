programmes

Healing

Art is the greatest form of expression of the human mind and body. Through history, it has been seen that art practices provide emotional and mental healing through its expressive powers. We work with women and children, to provide a platform for healing in order to lead more fulfilling lives.

Education

TAOS works with less privileged children, to introduce art in their lives. We aim to offer them an arena of creativity, discover freedom and enrich their minds, which provide strength for better performance at school and socially.

art education

Art provides a way for people to look into themselves and express their feelings.
For children, it can have additional benefits such as a boost in self-esteem. TAOS has worked
toward helping children unlock the true benefits of art through workshops in several schools.
